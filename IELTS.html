Challenge of the Week

Master the 4 IELTS skills

Listening

  • Predict answers before audio starts
  • Map speakers and tasks visually
  • Train accent range: UK, AUS, US

Reading

  • Skim for structure, scan for data
  • Master True/False/Not Given logic
  • Time-box each passage

Writing

  • Task 2: Thesis in 1 sentence
  • Paragraph = claim + evidence + link
  • Upgrade cohesion (topic sentences)

Speaking

  • Structure: concrete → abstract
  • Record + reflect on filler words
  • Natural lexical variety over memorization
